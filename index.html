About the Institution

Dr. Raj Shekhar Shrivastava Para Medical College is a dedicated academic institution established with the objective of strengthening the healthcare system through quality paramedical education. The college focuses on developing skilled healthcare support professionals capable of working efficiently in hospitals, laboratories, and medical institutions.

Academic Philosophy

The academic framework of the college emphasizes a balanced approach to learning, combining theoretical knowledge with structured practical training. Discipline, responsibility, and ethical conduct form the foundation of all academic activities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide structured, skill-oriented paramedical education that meets the evolving needs of the healthcare sector. The institution is committed to preparing competent professionals who uphold ethical standards and contribute positively to patient care.

Our Vision

Our vision is to emerge as a trusted center for paramedical education, recognized for academic integrity, practical excellence, and responsible healthcare training at the regional and national levels.

Core Values

The institution upholds values of discipline, integrity, respect for patients, academic honesty, and continuous professional development. These values guide both teaching and learning processes within the college.

Academic Programs Offered

The college offers structured paramedical programs designed to develop technical competence, clinical understanding, and professional responsibility required in healthcare services.

Diploma in Medical Laboratory Technology (DMLT)

This program focuses on laboratory diagnostics, pathology, hematology, microbiology, and medical testing procedures. Students receive hands-on training in laboratory equipment, sample analysis, and quality control practices.

Duration: 2 Years

Diploma in Operation Theatre Assistance (DOTA)

The program prepares students to assist surgeons and medical teams during surgical procedures. Training includes operation theatre protocols, sterilization methods, patient preparation, and surgical support techniques.

Duration: 2 Years

Diploma in Ophthalmic Assistance (DOA)

This course provides specialized training in eye care services, including ophthalmic diagnostics, clinical assistance, maintenance of eye care instruments, and patient management in ophthalmology departments.

Duration: 2 Years

Certificate in Medical Dressing (CMD)

The certificate program focuses on wound care management, dressing techniques, infection control, and basic nursing assistance required in hospitals and healthcare facilities.

Duration: 1 Year
